Biography

Yemi Laja

Founder & Director — Mastercraft Global Ltd

Yemi Laja is a multipotentialite with over 10 years of experience as a transformational coach, Mastercraft designer, UK Global Talent Endorsement specialist, theatre producer, and creative entrepreneur. She has delivered Mastercraft Coaching Conferences and programmes across the UK, Nigeria, and Ireland.

She is a valued member of the renowned Crescent Theatre in Birmingham — one of the UK's longest-standing theatres. Beyond performance, Yemi is a qualified lawyer (Barrister and Solicitor of the Federal Republic of Nigeria) and holds a Second Class Upper degree from Durham University.

This rare combination of legal precision, coaching methodology, creative expression, and UK Global Talent Endorsement expertise makes Mastercraft Global uniquely positioned to serve individuals and organisations at the highest level.
